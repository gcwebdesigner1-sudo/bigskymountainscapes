Our Story

Over 30 Years of
Big Sky Landscaping

Founded in 1992 by Doug Starz, Mountain Scapes has been shaping the outdoor spaces of Big Sky, West Yellowstone, and the surrounding Gallatin Valley for over three decades. We know this land — its soils, its seasons, its relentless winters and spectacular summers.

From stunning custom waterfalls and native plant gardens to hand-laid stonework and complete property transformations, every project is a collaboration between our experienced crew and the natural grandeur of Big Sky Country.

We specialize in water features — it's what we're known for, and it's what our clients love most. But whether it's a new patio, a retaining wall, or a complete landscape installation for a new construction home, we bring the same level of craftsmanship to every job.

Doug Starz
Founder · Mountain Scapes · Big Sky, MT

Our Process

Step 01
