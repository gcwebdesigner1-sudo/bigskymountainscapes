About Us

Born from the Beauty
of Big Sky Country

Nestled at the foot of some of Montana's most dramatic peaks, Big Sky Mountain Scapes has spent over a decade shaping the outdoor spaces of Big Sky's finest homes and properties. We understand this land — its soils, its seasons, its relentless winters and spectacular summers.

From sweeping mountain view terraces and native plant gardens to sophisticated irrigation systems and hand-laid stonework, every project is a collaboration between our expert team and the natural grandeur that surrounds us.

How It Works

Our Process

Every great landscape begins with a conversation. Here's how we bring your vision to life.

Step 01

Free Consultation

We visit your property, listen to your vision, and assess the site conditions, slope, soil, and sun exposure.

Step 02

Custom Design

Our designers produce detailed 3D renderings and planting plans tailored to your property's unique topography.

Step 03

Expert Installation

Our skilled crews execute every detail with precision — from grading and irrigation to planting and stonework.

Step 04

Ongoing Care

Seasonal maintenance programs ensure your landscape thrives through every Big Sky season, year after year.
